Why customers would care

The NIS does not just measure current. It helps users make safer, faster, and smarter decisions.

Safer monitoring

Because the sensor reads from outside the conductor, it reduces the need for direct contact with energized components.

Lower disruption

A non-intrusive design can reduce shutdowns, installation complexity, and interruptions to normal operation.

Better visibility

Real-time electrical data helps users spot inefficiency, overload risk, and maintenance needs earlier.

Who benefits?

  • Industrial facilities that want to monitor equipment health and reduce downtime.
  • Commercial buildings that need better energy visibility and safer installation options.
  • Utilities and maintenance teams looking for scalable monitoring without invasive retrofits.
  • Smart building systems that need reliable current data for automation and alerts.

Core value proposition

CurrentWise turns a complicated electrical measurement problem into a simpler customer promise: get useful current data with less disruption and more safety.

That message is important because customers do not buy technical details alone. They buy safer installation, easier deployment, lower risk, and clearer operational insight.
