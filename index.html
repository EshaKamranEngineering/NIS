Non-Intrusive Sensor Technology

See electrical current without cutting, touching, or shutting down the line.

CurrentWise uses a Non-Intrusive Sensor (NIS) to measure current from the outside of a conductor. That means faster installation, lower risk, and real-time data without interrupting service.

See how it works Why it matters

What problem are we solving?

Traditional sensing can be disruptive

Many current-monitoring methods require direct contact, line interruption, or complicated installation procedures.

Safety is a major concern

The closer technicians need to get to energized components, the greater the potential risk and the more training and downtime may be required.

Data is often delayed or limited

Without simple monitoring, problems may go unnoticed until energy waste, overheating, or equipment failure occurs.

Our technology approach

The NIS detects the electromagnetic field created by current flowing through a conductor. Because the sensor reads from the outside, it can collect useful electrical information while avoiding invasive installation.

In simple terms: current flows → a field is created → the NIS detects that field → the signal is converted into readable data.

Why we are different

We make electrical monitoring easier to understand and easier to deploy.
